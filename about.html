About Me

My name is Eduardo, I am currently studying Systems Analysis and Development at the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of São Paulo, Campus São Carlos. I'm 21 years old, and I've been involved in programming and data for 2 years.

As someone who is passionate about data analysis and data in general, I am also very drawn to the world of business intelligence. Data analysis is a fundamental part of business intelligence, as it is from there that we can extract valuable insights to help in decision making. Combining my skills in data analysis and PL/SQL development, I can help turn raw data into valuable information that can help companies better understand their business, identify growth opportunities, and make more strategic and informed decisions. For me, it is very rewarding to be able to work in the data field because I feel like I am helping to make a difference in business and ultimately in people's lives. It is an area that motivates me a lot and I hope to continue learning and developing my skills to become increasingly effective in helping companies use their data more intelligently.
